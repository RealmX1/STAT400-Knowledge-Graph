\section{Parameters}

\textbf{Has\_property:} \\
Expected value: 
\[
\mathbb{E}[X] = n \cdot \frac{M}{N}
\]

Variance:
\[
\text{Var}(X) = \frac{N - n}{N - 1} \cdot n \cdot \frac{M}{N} \cdot \left(1 - \frac{M}{N}\right)
\]

\textbf{Related\_to:} \\
Multivariate hypergeometric distribution, Binomial Distribution
