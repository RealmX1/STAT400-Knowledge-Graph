\section*{Binomial Distribution}

\section*{Properties}

1. The expected value of \( X \) is given by:
   \[
   \mathbb{E}(X) = n \cdot p
   \]

2. The variance of \( X \) is given by:
   \[
   \text{Var}(X) = n \cdot p \cdot (1 - p)
   \]
