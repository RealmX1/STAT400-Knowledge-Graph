The set of values \( X \) can take is finite or countably infinite, typically \(\{a, a+1, \ldots, b\}\), where \( a \) and \( b \) are the minimum and maximum.

\[
E[X] = \frac{a + b}{2}
\]

\[
\text{Var}(X) = \frac{(b - a + 1)^2 - 1}{12}
\]
